# Privacy Policy for Dot - Daily Check-In

**Last updated: January 7, 2026**

Dot - Daily Check-In ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind.

**We do not collect any personal data.** All your mood entries, habits, and notes are stored locally on your device only.

- Your data never leaves your device
- We have no servers and cannot access your information

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
