Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Dot - Daily Check-In ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your personal reflections should remain personal.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All your mood entries, habits, notes, and personal information are stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored exclusively on your device
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no servers and cannot access your information
  • If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store: If you purchase premium features, Apple processes the payment. We do not have access to your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
